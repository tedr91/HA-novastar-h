# Novastar H Series Integration

Control your Novastar H series LED video processors directly from Home Assistant.

## Features

- **Automatic Discovery** - Finds processors on your network via SSDP/Zeroconf
- **Power Control** - Turn the processor on/off
- **Output Control** - Enable/disable video output
- **Brightness Adjustment** - Control display brightness (0-100%)
- **Preset Selection** - Switch between configured presets
- **Media Player Entity** - Unified control interface

## Supported Devices

- Novastar H2
- Novastar H5
- Novastar H9
- Novastar H15
